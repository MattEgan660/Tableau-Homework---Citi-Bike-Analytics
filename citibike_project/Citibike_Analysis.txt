The most popular start and end stations based on the data are concentrated in Jersey City, especially around the water front areas.
Looking at the most popular stations, we can see that two of the of the top stations are light rail stops, indicating that a large portion
users, are using citibike as a means of "last mile" transportaion. This seems to be supported by the seasonal peak hours charts. Regardless of 
season, we start to see a sharp rise in session starts starting around 6am and peaks at 8am which falls inline with morning comute times. 
We also we the largest number of session starts at 6pm, which corresponds with peak rush hour.
